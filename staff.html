Dana Gepfrey, OTRL

Dana Gepfrey is the Executive Director (owner) and the Occupational Therapist. She has always dreamed of opening her own clinic and provide the best possible patient care. Dana has a passion for knowledge and will continue to research new treatment options for Autism and other pediatric conditions. Dana has a Master’s degree in Occupational Therapy along with a Bachelor of Science degree in Counseling Psychology. She specializes in pediatric and hand therapy and has extensive experience with sensory integration, developmental, and handwriting treatments. She also has training in oral motor, feeding, and torticollis treatments. In the adult population, Dana has experience in orthopedic and neurological conditions involving the upper extremities and abilities to perform activities of daily living. In her free time, she enjoys being outdoors, camping, swimming, and trying new arts and crafts.

Rebecca Hughes, SLP

Rebecca graduated from Oakland University with a bachelor’s in linguistics and received her master’s degree in speech-language pathology from Wayne State University. Rebecca is certified by the American Speech and Hearing Association, LSVT Loud certified, and trained in Language Acquisition through Motor Planning (LAMP). She is experienced in both adult and pediatric speech therapy, with extensive work with autism, apraxia, and augmentative/alternative communication. She is certified in the Listening Program. Rebecca enjoys spending time with her husband and two children, as well as the family’s two dogs.
